# Time-of-Use (TOU) Tariff Periods / فترات التعرفة حسب الوقت

## TOU Periods in Jordan

| Period | Arabic | Hours | Rate Level |
|--------|--------|-------|------------|
| Off-Peak | خارج الذروة | 05:00 - 14:00 | Cheapest |
| Partial Peak | ذروة جزئية | 14:00 - 17:00 and 23:00 - 05:00 | Medium |
| Peak | وقت الذروة | 17:00 - 23:00 | Most Expensive |

## EV Home Charging Rates (fils/kWh) / تعرفة شحن السيارات الكهربائية المنزلي

| Period | Arabic | Rate (fils/kWh) |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Off-Peak | خارج الذروة | 108 |
| Partial Peak | ذروة جزئية | 118 |
| Peak | وقت الذروة | 160 |

Savings by charging off-peak vs peak: up to 32% reduction in charging costs.

## EV Public Charging Station Rates (fils/kWh) / تعرفة محطات الشحن العامة

| Period | Arabic | Rate (fils/kWh) |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Off-Peak | خارج الذروة | 103 |
| Partial Peak | ذروة جزئية | 113 |
| Peak | وقت الذروة | 133 |

## Key Points / ملاحظات مهمة

- TOU currently applies to: EV charging, industrial, and water pumping tariffs.
- Residential tariffs still use the tiered (block) system, though TOU expansion is expected.
- 1 JOD = 1000 fils.
- Source: Energy and Minerals Regulatory Commission (EMRC) / هيئة تنظيم قطاع الطاقة والمعادن, effective July 2025.

## Practical Impact / التأثير العملي

A 7kW EV charger running for 4 hours:
- At peak (17:00-23:00): 7 kW x 4h x 160 fils = 4,480 fils (4.48 JOD)
- At off-peak (05:00-14:00): 7 kW x 4h x 108 fils = 3,024 fils (3.02 JOD)
- Daily savings: 1,456 fils (1.46 JOD)
- Monthly savings: approximately 43.7 JOD by shifting to off-peak charging
