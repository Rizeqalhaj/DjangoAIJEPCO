# JEPCO Residential Tariff Tiers / شرائح التعرفة السكنية

## Tiered Rate Structure / هيكل الشرائح

| Tier | kWh Range | Rate (fils/kWh) | Arabic |
|------|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | 0 - 160 | 33 | الشريحة الأولى |
| 2 | 161 - 320 | 72 | الشريحة الثانية |
| 3 | 321 - 480 | 86 | الشريحة الثالثة |
| 4 | 481 - 640 | 114 | الشريحة الرابعة |
| 5 | 641 - 800 | 158 | الشريحة الخامسة |

| Meter Type | Charge (fils) | Charge (JOD) |
|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| Single Phase (فاز واحد) | 500 | 0.50 |
| Three Phase (ثلاث فازات) | 1,500 | 1.50 |

## How Tiers Work / كيف تعمل الشرائح

The tariff is progressive (cumulative). Each kWh block is priced at its tier rate. Your bill is the sum of all tier charges plus the fixed monthly charge.

Example: 500 kWh monthly consumption:
- Tier 1: 160 kWh x 33 = 5,280 fils
- Tier 2: 160 kWh x 72 = 11,520 fils
- Tier 3: 160 kWh x 86 = 13,760 fils
- Tier 4: 20 kWh x 114 = 2,280 fils
- Energy charge: 32,840 fils
- Fixed charge (single phase): 500 fils
- Total: 33,340 fils = 33.34 JOD

## Tier Impact on Savings / تأثير الشرائح على التوفير

Small reductions in consumption can yield disproportionate savings because you drop from expensive tiers:

- 500 kWh/month: ~33.34 JOD
- 450 kWh/month: ~28.76 JOD
- Saving 50 kWh (10% less) saves ~4.58 JOD (13.7% cost reduction)

This is because the 50 kWh you save comes from the most expensive tier (Tier 4 at 114 fils/kWh), not the cheapest.

## Key Insight / ملاحظة مهمة

Every extra kWh gets more expensive. Reducing usage even slightly can drop you to a lower tier, saving more than you might expect. Focus on cutting peak-hour usage first.
